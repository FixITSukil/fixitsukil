3. Why We Collect Your Personal Data

In accordance with the Notice and Choice Principle under Section 7 of the PDPA, we inform you that your personal data is collected and processed for the following purposes:

  • To process and manage your repair booking or service request
  • To contact you regarding your repair status, quotation, or collection
  • To maintain service records and issue receipts or warranty documentation
  • To respond to enquiries submitted via our contact form or WhatsApp
  • To improve our website and services through anonymised analytics (Google Analytics)
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations under Malaysian law

We will not use your personal data for direct marketing without your explicit consent. You have the right to opt out of any marketing communication at any time by contacting us at [email protected].

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the General Principle (Section 6, PDPA), we process your personal data on the following bases:

  • Consent: By submitting a booking or contact form, you consent to us processing your data for service delivery purposes.
  • Contractual necessity: Processing is necessary to fulfil the repair service you have requested.
  • Legal obligation: We may process data where required to comply with applicable Malaysian laws.

5. How and Where We Store Your Data

Your personal data submitted through our booking and contact forms is transmitted to and stored in Google Sheets via a Google Apps Script web application. Google's servers may be located outside Malaysia. By submitting your data, you consent to this transfer.

We have assessed that Google LLC maintains data protection standards substantially similar to those required under the PDPA, including industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.

Website analytics data is processed by Google Analytics (GA4). This data is anonymised and aggregated and does not identify you personally.

Customer testimonials displayed on our website are sourced from Google Reviews via Elfsight, a third-party widget provider. Data displayed is limited to what you have publicly posted on Google.

6. Data Retention

In accordance with the Retention Principle (Section 10, PDPA), we retain your personal data only for as long as necessary:

  • Booking and service records are retained for a minimum of 2 years for warranty and dispute purposes.
  • Contact form enquiries that did not result in a service engagement are retained for 6 months and then deleted.
  • Analytics data is subject to Google's own retention policies (default 14 months in GA4).

Upon expiry of the retention period, data is deleted or anonymised securely.

7. Who We Share Your Data With

In accordance with the Disclosure Principle (Section 8, PDPA), we do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party. Your data may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Google LLC -- as our data storage and analytics processor (Google Sheets, Google Analytics)
  • Elfsight -- for displaying publicly available Google Reviews on our website
  • Law enforcement or regulatory authorities -- where we are legally required to disclose your data under Malaysian law

All third-party processors are contractually or by policy obligated to protect your data in accordance with applicable law.

8. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under the PDPA (as amended by the Personal Data Protection (Amendment) Act 2024), you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access (Section 30): You may request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right of Correction (Section 34): You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent (Section 38): You may withdraw your consent to the processing of your personal data at any time, subject to legal and contractual limitations.
  • Right to Prevent Processing for Direct Marketing (Section 43): You may instruct us to stop using your data for marketing purposes.
  • Right to Data Portability (Section 43A, effective June 2025): You may request that your personal data be transferred to another service provider, subject to technical feasibility.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us in writing at [email protected]. We will respond within 21 days of receiving your request.

9. Data Security

In accordance with the Security Principle (Section 9, PDPA), we take reasonable precautions to protect your personal data against loss, misuse, unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Measures include:

  • HTTPS encryption on this website, managed via Cloudflare
  • Access to Google Sheets service records restricted to authorised personnel only
  • No storage of payment card details -- payments are handled via bank transfer or third-party payment platforms

In the event of a personal data breach that is likely to cause significant harm, we will notify the Personal Data Protection Commissioner within 72 hours and notify affected individuals without unnecessary delay, in accordance with Section 12B of the amended PDPA.

10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies through Google Analytics (GA4). These are used solely to understand website traffic and improve user experience. No personally identifiable information is collected through cookies.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies will not affect your ability to book or use our services.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted personal data to us without parental consent, please contact us immediately at [email protected] and we will delete the data promptly.

Continued use of our website or services after any update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
