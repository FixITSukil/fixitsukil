🔧 Warranty & Policies

1. Repair Warranty

All repair services come with a standard warranty from the date of service completion, as follows:

Service Base Warranty
Hinge Repair 1 Month
Charging Port Repair 1 Month
Motherboard Repair 1 Month
Screen Replacement 1 Month
Battery Replacement 3 Months
Keyboard Replacement 1 Month
  • Warranty covers defects in workmanship and parts installed by Fix It Sukil only.
  • Does not cover physical damage caused after repair (e.g., new drops or spills).
  • Any unauthorized opening of the chassis or tampering voids the warranty.

1a. Extended Warranty Add-On

Extend your repair warranty beyond the standard period. Add-on must be purchased before or at the time of device collection. Flat rate applies to all services.

Extension Period Add-On Price (RM) Total Coverage
+3 Months RM 80 4 months (or 6 months for Battery)
+6 Months RM 120 7 months (or 9 months for Battery)
+1 Year RM 150 13 months (or 15 months for Battery)
  • Extended warranty covers the same scope as the standard warranty -- workmanship and installed parts only.
  • Extended warranty add-on is non-refundable once purchased.

1b. Parts & Manufacturer Warranty

Replacement parts sourced and installed by Fix It Sukil carry a manufacturer warranty where applicable:

  • Manufacturer warranty ranges from 1 month to 10 years depending on the part and brand.
  • Warranty terms are set by the manufacturer and are separate from Fix It Sukil's service warranty.
  • Proof of purchase and part details will be provided upon request.
  • Manufacturer warranty does not cover accidental damage, misuse, or liquid damage after installation.

2. Payment & Refund Policy

  • We accept bank transfers (Maybank, CIMB, etc.) or cash on collection.
  • Full payment is due only after service completion, unless otherwise arranged.
  • If you cancel before pickup, no charges will apply.
    If cancellation occurs after we've picked up the device, a RM 30 inspection fee applies.

No-Refund Policy

  • Service charges (labour) are non-refundable once the repair has been completed and the device returned to the customer.
  • Extended warranty add-on fees are non-refundable once purchased.
  • Pickup and delivery fees are non-refundable once the trip has been made. Standard pickup and delivery for repairs is offered as a goodwill gesture at no charge, however a fee applies when the service is used for warranty claim visits.

Refund Exception

A refund of the original service charge will be considered only if all of the following conditions are met:

  • The same fault recurs within the active warranty period.
  • The customer has submitted a warranty claim and Fix It Sukil has made all reasonable attempts to rectify the fault.
  • All available warranty claim options have been exhausted and the fault remains unresolved.

In such cases, a refund of the service charge only will be issued. Parts costs, pickup/delivery fees, and extended warranty add-on fees remain non-refundable.

3. Data Privacy & Liability

  • We handle your data with utmost care—no software or personal files are accessed without permission.
  • While we take all precautions, we are not liable for data loss. We strongly recommend you back up important files before service.
  • In the unlikely event of further hardware damage during service, our liability is limited to the original repair fee.

4. Pickup & Delivery Terms

  • We offer free pickup/delivery across Greater Klang Valley (within a 30 km radius).
  • We aim to pick up your device within 24 hours of booking confirmation.
  • Delivery is typically made within 2 business days of repair completion.
